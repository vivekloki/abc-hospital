Dental Surgery

A healthy set of pearly whites make your smile look good! The Department of Dental Surgery is well known for their dental services. The doctors from the field of dentistry are well experienced in their area of work and provide the best of treatments for you. An expert team of dentists also cater to dental implants, facial fractures and trauma related cases. The Department also specializes in general dental care, root canal therapy and treatment for gums and supporting tissues

General Practices:

  • Dental Radiography
  • Scaling
  • Teeth restorations, root Canal Treatment
  • Placement of removable and fixed dentures
  • Placement of oral implants

    • Oral & maxillofacial Surgery:

  • Simple and difficult tooth extraction
  • Incision & drainage of abscesses
  • Treatment of fractures of face and jaws
  • Treatment of cysts and tumours oral cavity and jaws
  • Incisional and excision biopsies
  • Treatment of disorders related to the temporomandibular joints
  • Treatment of various diseases of the oral cavity to usage of tobacco

Orthodontics:

It deals with both children and adults patients having jaw deformities, maligned teeth, impacted teeth, patients with smile correction. Procedures which can be performed are-

  • Fixed orthodontics which can be either using metal brackets or ceramic brackets.
  • Invisible orthodontics using lingual braces or clear aligners
  • Myofunctional orthopaedics in children to correct different malocclusion and jaw deformities.
  • Orthognathic surgeries
