CANCELLATION POLICY FOR DOCTOR APPOINTMENT

We understand that there are times when you must miss an appointment due to emergencies or obligations for work or family. However, when you do not call to cancel an appointment, you may be preventing another patient from getting much needed treatment. Conversely, the situation may arise where another patient fails to cancel and other patients are unable to schedule for a visit, due to a seemingly “full” appointment book. If an appointment is not cancelled at least 24 hours in advance you will be not refunded.
