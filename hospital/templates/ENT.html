ENT

The Department of ENT at MAJ Hospital has started in 1982 and has a range of ingenious treatments which cater to the patients suffering with diseases related to the ear, nose and throat. We aim at providing the best service possible. We have state-of-the-art operation theatre and super specialized doctors in head & neck surgery. At MAJ we:

  • Diagnostic nasal endoscopy
  • Laryngoscopy
  • All minor procedures
  • 24 hours emergency services
  • Management of nasal bleeding
  • Management of foreign bodies of ear
  • Tracheotomy
  • Management of facial injuries
